The First Appeal: outliving the feed

A new beginning for Appeal of AI: less noise, more signal, and a website that becomes the memory layer for everything published on X.

Contents Why this exists The format Key takeaways X draft

Why this exists

The AI feed is fast, but speed is not the same thing as understanding. Appeal of AI is built around a simple editorial question: what deserves attention once the announcement, demo, or outrage has already passed?

The name carries two meanings. There is the appeal of AI as attraction: the images, tools, systems, and strange new creative power. And there is appeal as argument: the case for, against, and around intelligent systems.

The website is the archive. X is the stage.

The format

Each article should be useful on its own, but also easy to turn into a native X post, thread, or long-form X Article. That keeps the public conversation close to the feed while preserving a canonical version on the site.

Key takeaways

  • Use the website for durable essays, source notes, and visual identity.
  • Use X for hooks, arguments, excerpts, and distribution.
  • Keep the NFT origin as future archive material, not the first relaunch message.

What comes next

The first live pieces can be short: one release, one claim, one debate, one visual artifact. The important part is consistency of voice: curious, skeptical, aesthetically aware, and specific.
