Title: Vibe coding is leverage, not a replacement for judgment.

Vibe coding is a useful cultural phrase. The more precise term is AI-assisted
programming.

Without expertise, the workflow is risky. A model can produce code that runs,
but a beginner may not see the structural mistake, missing edge case, weak
security boundary or architecture that will become painful later.

With expertise, the same workflow becomes leverage. It speeds up drafts,
branches, refactors and exploration. The human still needs taste, review and
technical judgment.

The appeal is simple: craft suddenly has a much faster instrument.
