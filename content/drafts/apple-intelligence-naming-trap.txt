Title: Apple Intelligence still has a naming problem.
Type: Article draft
Date: 2026-06-09
Status: Draft

Angle:
Apple may not have needed a louder AI brand. It needed delivery so quiet and
reliable that the name became obvious later.

Draft:
Apple Intelligence is a beautiful name and maybe that is the problem.

It sounds inevitable. It sounds like the operating system finally learned how to
understand you. But when Siri still misses ordinary tasks, the name turns every
small failure into a brand-level event.

Apple usually wins by making technology feel calm. AI, at least for now, has
made Apple feel slightly over-announced. The appeal is still there: private,
device-native intelligence that does not feel like a chatbot bolted to the side
of your phone. But that appeal depends on delivery. If the assistant is late,
the name gets expensive.
