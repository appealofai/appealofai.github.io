Title: The AI bubble question is really an infrastructure question.

I do not buy the simple "AI is a bubble" take. Some valuations can be
overheated, and some demos can be theater. But the deeper story is physical:
power, cooling, chips, land, grids and data centers.

The sharper question is whether the infrastructure boom can turn all that demand
into enough useful intelligence. The future is not just bigger models. It is
useful intelligence per watt.
