## Needs stronger verification before publishing

- "US government bans Fable 5 and Mythos 5": no publication without a primary government or company source. Treat as unverified.
- "Electricity costs rose 267%": no publication as a general AI claim unless the exact location, period, methodology and source are known.
- "Meta already tens of billions for token use": verify whether the claim refers to capex, data-center buildout, inference serving cost, training compute, or third-party cloud commitments. These are not the same story.
- "Zuckerberg admits Meta made mistakes with AI workforce shift": publish only if there is a direct interview, letter, transcript or official post.
